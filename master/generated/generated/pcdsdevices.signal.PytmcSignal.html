pcdsdevices.signal.PytmcSignal¶

class pcdsdevices.signal.PytmcSignal(prefix, io=None, **kwargs)¶

Class for a connection to a pytmc-generated EPICS record.

This uses the same args as the pragma, so you can refer to the pytmc pragmas to select args for your components. This will automatically append the ‘_RBV’ suffix and wrap the read/write PVs into the same signal object as appropriate, and pick between a read-only signal and a writable one.

Under the hood this actually gives you the RW or RO version of the signal depending on your io argument.
