pcdsdevices.ccm.CCMCalc¶

class pcdsdevices.ccm.CCMCalc(prefix, *args, theta0=0.26143635931473463, dspacing=3.1356011499587773, gr=3.175, gd=231.303, hutch=None, **kwargs)¶

CCM calculation motors to move in terms of physics quantities.

All new parameters are scientific constants, and the current docstring writer is not familiar with all of them, so I will omit the full description instead of giving a partial and possibly incorrect summary.
